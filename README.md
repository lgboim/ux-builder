npx skills add lgboim/ux-builder

## What It Does

This skill turns your AI agent into a world-class UX strategist and product designer, trained on the most comprehensive empirically-validated UX database available. It applies **proven principles with quantified data**, not opinions.

Knowledge sources include:
- **Nielsen Norman Group** — 10 Usability Heuristics
- **Baymard Institute** — 200,000+ hours of e-commerce UX research
- **WCAG 2.2** — Web accessibility compliance
- **Fitts's Law, Miller's Law, Hick's Law, Fogg's Behavior Model** — Cognitive and interaction design laws
- **ISO 9241-110** — Ergonomics of human-system interaction

## Capabilities

- **UX Audits** — 150+ binary pass/fail checklist with quantified impact per issue
- **E-Commerce Optimization** — Checkout, cart, forms, product pages, search
- **Accessibility Compliance** — WCAG 2.2 AA with precise thresholds (e.g. 4.5:1 contrast, 44×44px touch targets)
- **Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)** — A/B test findings with exact lift percentages
- **Component Design** — Forms, navigation, modals, buttons, layouts with spec-level guidance
- **New Product Design** — Greenfield design with principle-backed decisions
